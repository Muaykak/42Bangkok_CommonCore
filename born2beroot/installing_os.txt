
Hostname

	- The hostname is a single word that indentifies your system to the
	network.

Domain name

	- The domain name is the part of your Internet address to the right
	of your host name. It is often something that ends in .com, .net ,
	.edu, or .org.

root

	- 'root' is the system administrative account. A malicious or
	unqualified user with root access can have disastrous results
	- the 'root' user should not have an empty password.
